## Highlights

### ✓ Positives

1. **Revenue Beat Driven by AI Suite**: AI revenue grew 85% YoY to $45M (27% of total), significantly ahead of our $38M estimate
2. **Margin Expansion**: Gross margin expanded 250bps to 74.5% on product mix shift and scale
3. **Guidance Raised**: FY26 revenue guidance increased to $680-700M (from $650-670M)
4. **Strong Bookings**: Q4 bookings up 35% YoY; RPO grew 42% to $520M
5. **Cash Generation**: FCF margin of 23.3% demonstrates strong unit economics

### ⚠ Concerns

1. **Customer Concentration**: Top 3 customers = 28% of revenue (up from 24% last quarter)
2. **Competition**: Mentioned increased pricing pressure in mid-market segment
3. **Hiring Costs**: S&M expense grew faster than revenue (+35% YoY)

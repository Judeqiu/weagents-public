# Conversation Cheat Sheet

Exact phrases to use when talking to the AI.

---

## 🎯 Valuation ("What's it worth?")

### Quick Valuation
**You:** "Value [COMPANY NAME] - it's a [TYPE] company with $[X]M revenue, $[Y]M profit. Competitors are [A, B, C]."

**Example:**
> "Value TechFlow - it's a software company with $65M revenue, $15M profit. Competitors are Salesforce, Adobe, and HubSpot."

**AI Response:** 
- Creates Excel file
- Runs analysis
- Tells you: "Based on comparables, worth $480-520M"

---

### DCF Valuation (Future-based)
**You:** "What's [COMPANY] worth based on future cash flows? They make $[X]M now, growing [Y]% per year."

**Example:**
> "What's TechFlow worth based on future cash flows? They make $15M now, growing 15% per year."

**AI Response:**
- Builds 5-year projection
- Calculates present value
- Tells you: "Worth $485M based on 15% growth"

---

### Simple Business Valuation
**You:** "What would a [BUSINESS TYPE] making $[X] per year sell for?"

**Example:**
> "What would a coffee shop making $100k profit per year sell for?"

**AI Response:**
- Industry multiples
- Quick range: "Coffee shops sell for 3-5× profit, so $300-500k"

---

## 🎯 Finding Buyers ("Who wants to buy it?")

### Find Buyers
**You:** "Who would want to buy my [INDUSTRY] company? We make $[X]M revenue, $[Y]M profit."

**Example:**
> "Who would want to buy my software company? We make $50M revenue, $10M profit."

**AI Response:**
- Generates CSV list
- Tier 1: Best fits
- Tier 2: Maybe interested
- Tier 3: Probably not

---

### Strategic vs Financial Buyers
**You:** "Find both strategic acquirers and PE firms for [DESCRIPTION]."

**Example:**
> "Find both strategic acquirers and PE firms for a healthcare IT company with $30M revenue."

**AI Response:**
- Separates strategic (companies) vs financial (PE firms)
- Explains rationale for each

---

## 🎯 Stock Analysis ("Should I buy?")

### Earnings Analysis
**You:** "[COMPANY] just reported earnings. What do you think?"

**Example:**
> "Apple just reported earnings. What do you think?"

**AI Response:**
- Beat or miss expectations
- Key highlights
- Recommendation

---

### Buy/Hold/Sell Decision
**You:** "Should I buy [STOCK] at $[PRICE]?"

**Example:**
> "Should I buy Tesla at $200?"

**AI Response:**
- Bull case (upside)
- Bear case (downside)
- Base case (fair value)
- Clear recommendation

---

### Compare Two Stocks
**You:** "Which is better: [STOCK A] or [STOCK B]?"

**Example:**
> "Which is better: Apple or Microsoft right now?"

**AI Response:**
- Side-by-side comparison
- Growth, valuation, risks
- Winner recommendation

---

### Portfolio Tracking
**You:** "Track my portfolio: [STOCK1], [STOCK2], [STOCK3]. Alert me to important news."

**Example:**
> "Track my portfolio: AAPL, TSLA, NVDA. Alert me to important news."

**AI Response:**
- Sets up monitoring
- Tells you upcoming events
- Flags risks/opportunities

---

## 🎯 LBO/Private Equity ("Can we make money?")

### Quick LBO Check
**You:** "Can we make money buying [BUSINESS] for $[PRICE]? They make $[X] profit per year."

**Example:**
> "Can we make money buying a car wash chain for $5M? They make $800k profit per year."

**AI Response:**
- Calculates return
- Tells you: "2.2× your money in 5 years = 17% annually"
- Verdict: Good deal or not

---

### Full LBO Analysis
**You:** "Run a full LBO model on [COMPANY]. Buy for $[X], use $[Y] equity, $[Z] debt. Exit in [N] years."

**Example:**
> "Run a full LBO model on a manufacturing company. Buy for $50M, use $20M equity, $30M debt. Exit in 5 years."

**AI Response:**
- Creates Excel LBO model
- Debt paydown schedule
- Returns calculation
- Sensitivity analysis

---

### Investment Memo
**You:** "Write an investment memo for buying [BUSINESS TYPE]."

**Example:**
> "Write an investment memo for buying a dental practice chain."

**AI Response:**
- Full IC memo structure
- Market analysis
- Financial projections
- Risk factors

---

## 🎯 Follow-Up Questions

### Get Explanation
**You:** "How did you calculate that?"

**AI Response:**
- Step-by-step breakdown
- Shows assumptions

---

### Change Assumptions
**You:** "What if [ASSUMPTION] is different?"

**Example:**
> "What if growth is only 10% instead of 15%?"

**AI Response:**
- Updates model
- New valuation/return

---

### Best/Worst Case
**You:** "Show me best case, worst case, and base case."

**AI Response:**
- Three scenarios
- Range of outcomes
- Probability discussion

---

### Get the File
**You:** "Send me the Excel file" or "Give me the document"

**AI Response:**
- Provides file path
- Explains what each sheet shows

---

### Shorter Summary
**You:** "Give me the short version" or "Summarize in 3 bullet points"

**AI Response:**
- Condensed answer
- Key takeaways only

---

### More Detail
**You:** "Explain that more" or "Walk me through your thinking"

**AI Response:**
- Detailed explanation
- Shows methodology

---

## 🎯 Complete Conversation Examples

### Scenario 1: Selling Your Business

**You:** Help me sell my software company.

**AI:** Tell me about it - revenue, profit, and what type of software?

**You:** $25M revenue, $5M profit. It's project management software.

**AI:** [Finds buyers] Here are 30 potential buyers. Tier 1 includes Atlassian, Monday.com, and several PE firms.

**You:** What's a fair price?

**AI:** [Runs valuation] Based on comparables, $100-125M is fair. I can create a full valuation report.

**You:** Create a teaser for Tier 1 buyers.

**AI:** [Writes teaser] Here's a draft...

---

### Scenario 2: Investment Decision

**You:** Is Netflix a buy at $600?

**AI:** Let me analyze. [Does analysis]

**AI:** It's fairly valued. Bull case $750, bear case $450. My view: HOLD at current levels.

**You:** What are the main risks?

**AI:** Competition from Disney+, password sharing crackdown impact, content costs rising...

**You:** Compare to Disney.

**AI:** Disney cheaper but slower growth. Netflix better business but priced in. I'd pick Netflix for growth, Disney for value.

---

### Scenario 3: Buying a Business

**You:** Thinking of buying a laundromat for $300k. Good idea?

**AI:** What are the annual profits?

**You:** About $60k per year.

**AI:** [Quick calculation] That's 5× profit, which is fair. Returns depend on growth. If stable, you're making 20% cash-on-cash. Decent but not amazing.

**You:** Can you improve it?

**AI:** Ideas: Add pickup/delivery service, install card readers, vending machines. Could increase profits 30-50%.

**You:** Run the numbers on improved scenario.

**AI:** [Updates model] With improvements, $90k profit, sell for $450k in 5 years. That's 2.5× return, 20% annually. Much better!

## Tips

✅ **Be specific** - Include revenue, profit, industry  
✅ **Name competitors** - Helps the AI find comparables  
✅ **Ask follow-ups** - "What if...", "Why...", "How..."  
✅ **Request files** - "Send me the model"  
❌ **Don't be vague** - "Analyze this company" (which company?)  
❌ **Don't forget numbers** - The AI needs data to work with  

---

**Just talk naturally!** The AI will ask for whatever information it needs.

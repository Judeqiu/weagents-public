# Monthly Full Audit Workflow

**Duration:** 2-3 hours  
**Frequency:** 1st weekend of each month  
**Trigger:** Student says "Monthly audit"

## Purpose

The monthly full audit provides a comprehensive health check of your exam preparation. It refreshes resources, analyzes all subjects, and generates a progress report with updated targets for the coming month.

## Session Structure

```
Total Time: 2-3 hours (can split across weekend)

Step 1: Resource Refresh (30 min)
Step 2: Full Weakness Hunter (90 min)
Step 3: Progress Report Generation (30 min)
Step 4: Next Month Planning (15 min)
```

## Post-Audit Actions

1. **Save progress report** to tracking folder
2. **Update all tracking templates** with new data
3. **Set calendar reminder** for next month's audit
4. **Share report** with tutor/parent if desired
5. **Celebrate wins** - you've done the work!

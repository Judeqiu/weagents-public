## Best Practices Reminder

### 1-Week Review (Consolidation)
- Focus on questions where you lost marks
- 20-30 minutes
- Open notes allowed
- Goal: Fix the gaps

### 3-Week Review (True Mastery Test)
- Full re-attempt, closed book
- Timed (exam conditions)
- 75-90 minutes
- Goal: 85%+ to prove mastery

### 6-Week Review (Permanent Encoding)
- Full re-attempt, exam conditions
- Strict timing
- Goal: 90%+ for exam readiness

---

## Spacing Mindset

> "3 papers with proper spacing > 10 papers without review"

Remember:
- ✅ Space it out (1-3-6 weeks)
- ✅ Test true mastery (closed book)
- ✅ Track retention (don't assume)
- ✅ Fix drops immediately (don't ignore)
